# 🎓 Senior 2027 Poster Generator – FCAI CU

A browser-based poster generator built for students of the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence, Cairo University (FCAI CU).

This project allows students to create personalized **Senior 2027 graduation posters** by uploading a portrait photo, entering their name, selecting their department, and generating a ready-to-download poster instantly.

---

## 📖 Usage Guide

Follow these steps to create your Senior 2027 poster:

### 1. Launch the Application
Open the poster generator in your browser.

You will see:
- Template selection panel
- Photo upload area
- Name input field
- Department selector
- Live preview canvas

---

### 2. Choose a Template
Select your preferred poster design from the available templates:

- Dark Neon
- Navy & Gold
- Pastel

Each template has a different visual style.

---

### 3. Prepare Your Photo
Choose a clear portrait photo where:

✅ Face is centered  
✅ Lighting is good  
✅ Background is simple (preferred)  
✅ Image quality is high  

Recommended:
- Square or portrait image
- High resolution image

---

### 4. Optional: Create a Graduation Photo
If your image does not include graduation attire:

1. Upload your photo to :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
2. Use the built-in graduation prompt helper
3. Generate a graduation portrait
4. Download and use the edited image

Recommended attire:
- Graduation robe
- Graduation cap
- White background

---

### 5. Upload Your Photo
Click the **Photo Upload** area.

Supported formats:
- JPG
- PNG
- WEBP

After upload:
- Thumbnail preview appears
- Image becomes ready for poster generation

---

### 6. Enter Your Name
Type your full name.

Example:

```text
Mohammed Salah
```

Your name will automatically appear on the poster.

---

### 7. Select Department
Choose your department from the dropdown list:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Computer Science
- Information Systems
- Information Technology
- Decision Support

The selected department will be displayed on the poster.

---

### 8. Generate Poster
Click:

```text
✨ Generate Poster
```

The application will:
- Load selected template
- Crop and position your image
- Render your name
- Render your department
- Update live preview

---

### 9. Preview Result
Check the generated poster inside the preview area.

Verify:
- Photo position
- Name spelling
- Department selection
- Overall appearance

---

### 10. Optional: Download Poster
Click:

```text
⬇ Download PNG
```

The poster will be downloaded as a high-quality PNG image.

You can use it for:
- Social media posts
- Graduation announcements
- Printing
- Senior event promotions

---

### 11. Send The Poster
Click:

```text
Submit Poster
```

The poster will be uploaded to the drive so we can use it in the parties
--- 

## ⚠ Best Practices

For best poster quality:

- Use HD images
- Avoid blurry photos
- Use short readable names if possible
- Prefer white or simple backgrounds
- Check spelling before download

---

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### Image not loading?
Check:
- File format
- File size
- Browser compatibility

### Poster looks blurry?
Use higher resolution image.

### Download button disabled?
Generate the poster first.
